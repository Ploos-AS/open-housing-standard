# 3. Normative References

## 3.1 General

The documents listed in this section are referenced such that parts of
their content constitute requirements of this specification.

For dated references, only the cited edition applies. For undated
references, the latest published edition applies.

## 3.2 RFC 2119

RFC 2119 defines the interpretation of the normative keywords MUST, MUST
NOT, REQUIRED, SHALL, SHALL NOT, SHOULD, SHOULD NOT, RECOMMENDED, MAY,
and OPTIONAL.

## 3.3 RFC 8174

OHS adopts the clarification provided by RFC 8174 regarding
interpretation of normative language.

## 3.4 Future Normative References

Future OHS specifications may introduce additional normative references
related to BIM, accessibility, networking, energy performance,
documentation, and open file formats.
